Predicting Company Bankruptcy and Financial Status Analysis Using Machine Learning and Deep Learning

This project, predicts inflation rates based on the following factors: CPI Value, Currency against USD, Money Supply, Interest Rates, GDP (in BDT) and GDP Per Capita. This research used four machine learning models and four deep learning models for predicting future rates. The best machine learning model for forecasting inflation was Random Forest with an R-squared score of 0.96 and the best deep learning model was found to be a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) with an accuracy of 0.94.

Data Driven Approach to Forecasting Inflation in Bangladesh

The research uses machine learning techniques to predict the chances that a company goes bankrupt based on various factors. For predicting the bankruptcy, we have used various machine learning techniques: SVM, XGBoost, Random Forest, Logistic Regression and Decision Tree. On performing our experiments with the models, we found Random Forest to be the best technique for predicting bankruptcy. The model produced a Training accuracy of 99.1%. We also applied different deep learning methods such as DNN, GRU, TCN to compare ML models performance. We found that each of these 3 DL models produce a training accuracy of approximately 98%. Bankruptcy has been on the rise over the past decade and it is very important to predict and take precautions for a business.

Arduino based solar tracker

The project explores an Arduino-based solar tracker, a device designed to enhance solar energy absorption by maintaining the optimal angle with the sun. By utilizing light-dependent resistors (LDRs) for automatic control, this dual-axis tracker adjusts the solar panel's position throughout the day. The project aims to provide an open hardware/software test bench for students to understand control theory and its practical applications. This solar tracking system demonstrates increased efficiency compared to static panels, leading to higher current, voltage, and power outputs. Overall, it showcases the benefits of a solar tracker in reducing electricity costs and optimizing solar energy utilization.
